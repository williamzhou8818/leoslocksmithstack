SERVICES

OPENING HOUSE DOORS

Gain entry to premises when keys are lost or left inside, ID check required. Locksmithing skills are applied for a quick, smooth and no-damage opening except for extreme situations.

OPENING CAR DOORS

Gain entry to cars when keys are lost or left inside, often left in ignition lock or boot. Car lock opening skills are applied to avoid or minimize any forcing on car doors.

ALTERING LOCKS

Change the cylinders of locks with new keys in order to stop the old keys working.

CAR KEYS DUPLICATING, GENERATING AND PROGRAMMING

We supply car keys including plan keys, transponder keys and transponder remote keys. They vary from blade keys to foldable flip keys. A spare transponder key can become a day saver. Either you already have a working key or lose all keys, we can help generate a key and it starts the engine.

LOCK INSTALLATION AND REPAIRING

We fit locks supplied both by us and customers. As for broken locks we provide professional advice followed by new solutions or repairing.

ALTERING AND REPAIRING CAR LOCKS

Broken door or ignition locks can be a nightmare. They jam keys in it or not turn on the engine. We can help rebuild or replace door or ignition locks and alter them to your existing working keys.

ANTIQUE LOCK REFURNISHING

If you have an old, rare or odd-looking lock to restore, repair or make a key to, we definitely can help you by delicate, specialized and creative skills to meet your specific requirements.
